## Weekly Focus: Finalization

As the hackathon enters its final weeks, it's time to begin with the end in mind.

Finalization is not just about finishing a project. It's about understanding the story of what you've built.

If you had to give your final presentation today:

- What story would you tell?
  
- What evidence would you show?
  
- What outcomes would you highlight?
  
- What is still missing?
  

As you answer those questions, you'll discover where to focus next.

Remember: clarity beats complexity.

A strong presentation does not try to show everything. It helps the audience quickly understand the problem, the solution, the evidence, and the value created.

The Piece of Pie final presentation requirements are intentionally simple:

- Demonstrate your product live
  
- Share the official public repository
  
- Share the deployed public product
  
- Include links to the required weekly updates
  

That's it.

Additional screenshots, metrics, user interviews, testimonials, and supporting evidence are optional. They can strengthen your presentation and help tell a clearer story, but they are not additional requirements.

Builders pursuing the Cardano Pie should also remember that qualified teams will have a chance to pitch to the Draper Dragon Venture Fund as part of the opportunity sponsored by the Cardano Foundation.

Whether you're presenting to fellow builders, potential users, or potential investors, the same principle applies:

**Make it Clear**
