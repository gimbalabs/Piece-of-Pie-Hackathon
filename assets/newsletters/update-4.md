## ![🔧](https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/17.0/1f527/72.png) Focus for Next Week: Finding Your Builder Rhythm

Registration is done. You’re in. Now the real building begins.

Over the next week, one of the most important things you can do is start figuring out your builder routine.

What does your rhythm look like?

- Do you build in the morning or late at night?

- Is this part of your day job, or something you fit in around life?

- Do you work mostly on weekends?

- Do you prefer short daily sessions or longer focused blocks?

- Is your current approach sustainable?

There’s no single correct way to do this.

This 12-week hackathon is partly an experiment in learning how you 
work best. Use this time to test routines, adjust your habits, and 
discover a pace you can maintain consistently.

It's a marathon, not a sprint.

The goal is steady progress.

Keep moving forward, keep sharing updates weekly, and keep building momentum one week at a time.
