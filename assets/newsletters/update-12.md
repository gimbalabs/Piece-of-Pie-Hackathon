## Weekly Focus: Ship and Share

The finish line is finally in sight.

A deadline marks both an ending and a beginning. It brings one chapter to a close while opening the door to whatever comes next.

This week is about finishing strong.

Resist the temptation to chase perfection. Instead, focus on 
completing the work that matters most. Remove features that aren't 
essential. Fix the biggest issues. Tell the story of what you built and 
why it matters.

As you prepare your final presentation, take a moment to look back 
through your weekly updates, GitHub commits, feedback sessions, and 
conversations with users. They tell the story of your journey—what you 
learned, what changed, and how your project evolved.

Celebrate that progress.

Every builder has something worth sharing, whether it's a polished 
application, a valuable lesson, a new skill, or a clearer understanding 
of the problem they're trying to solve.

The final presentation is not the end of your project. It is your first opportunity to share it with a wider audience.

Some projects will continue beyond the hackathon. Others will inspire your next idea. Both are successful outcomes.

A finished product teaches more than a perfect idea.

**Ship and share.**
