# Overview

## What Piece of Pie Is

Piece of Pie is a 12-week, participation-driven hackathon organized by Gimbalabs.

The purpose is to create a lightweight structure that encourages people to:

- build consistently
- share progress publicly
- test ideas in the real world
- learn in public with clear expectations

This hackathon is not designed around judges ranking winners. It is designed around participants meeting public, verifiable requirements.

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver

To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems.

## Proposal Driver

The hackathon exists to address a simple problem:

- builders often work in isolation
- progress is hard to see
- there is little push toward real users

Piece of Pie responds with a low-friction structure that rewards:

- consistent participation
- public progress
- real-world outcomes

## Format

- Duration: 12 weeks
- Enrollment period: first 2 weeks only
- Enrollment closes after Week 2
- One participant may only join one project
- Projects may be solo or team-based
- Every contributor must be named during registration

## Key Principle

Qualify through consistent, verifiable participation, not competition.

## What Participants Should Expect

Participants should expect a format built around:

- one official public repository per project
- visible public progress every week
- a final presentation used for verification
- clear qualification requirements for each reward track

## What This Repo Covers

This repository documents:

- the public rules
- the 2026 timeline
- the reward structure
- registration expectations
- verification requirements
- public clarifications and future formal decisions
