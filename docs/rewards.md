# Rewards

## Reward Pools

The 2026 hackathon reward structure is divided into separate pools, referred to as pies.

- Builder Pie: 10,000 ADA
- Real User Pie: 2,000 ADA
- Feedback Pie: 1,000 ADA
- Administration: 3,000 ADA

## Builder Pie

The Builder Pie is split equally among all participants who fully qualify for the Builder track.

Key idea:

- this is an equal split pool
- qualification matters more than ranking

## Real User Pie

The Real User Pie is split equally among participants who:

- qualify as builders first
- also meet the Real User requirements

## Feedback Pie

The Feedback Pie is distributed proportionally based on feedback credits.

Rules:

- 1 live, recorded feedback session = 1 credit
- a participant can earn up to 15 credits
- the 1,000 ADA pool is divided proportionally across all valid credits

## Feedback Example

If:

- Alice earns 15 credits
- Bill earns 5 credits
- Charles earns 10 credits

Then:

- total credits = 30
- 1,000 / 30 = 33.33 ADA per credit, subject to final rounding decisions

Estimated payouts:

- Alice: 15 x 33.33
- Bill: 5 x 33.33
- Charles: 10 x 33.33

## Administration

An additional 3,000 ADA is allocated for administrative support, including:

- coordination
- participant tracking
- website and enrollment flow support
- final presentation logistics

## Important Note

Reward pools are distributed based on qualification and verification, not on subjective judging.
