# Real User Rules

## Real User Pie Summary

The Real User Pie rewards projects that go beyond building and demonstrate real-world paid usage.

Real User Pie allocation:

- 2,000 ADA
- equal split among qualified participants

## Requirements

To qualify for the Real User Pie, a participant or project must meet all of the following requirements.

### 1. Qualify as a Builder First

Real User qualification depends on Builder qualification.

If a project does not qualify for the Builder Pie, it does not qualify for the Real User Pie.

### 2. Secure at Least One Paying User

The project must secure at least one real paying user.

Family and friends do not meet this requirement.

### 3. Provide Evidence of Payment

Participants must provide verifiable evidence that a real customer paid for the product.

Accepted evidence can come from any payment method, including:

- payment receipts
- invoices marked paid
- billing dashboard records
- bank transfer records
- app store or platform payout records
- blockchain transaction records

### 4. Provide a Customer Acquisition Story

Participants must explain how the paying user was acquired.

The purpose is to show real-world outreach and validation, not just payment evidence in isolation.

### 5. Share the Evidence Publicly

The payment evidence and customer acquisition story must be shared publicly for verification.

The evidence must clearly show:

- a payment happened
- the amount paid
- the date of payment
- that the payment was for the product
- enough context to show it came from a real customer

For blockchain payments, include the transaction record and enough supporting context to show it was a real customer purchase, not just a wallet transfer.

## Practical Standard

The Real User Pie is meant to reward real-world validation, not only shipping.
