# Cardano Pie Rules

## Cardano Pie Summary

The Cardano Pie rewards qualifying projects that satisfy the Builder Pie requirements and ship real functionality on Cardano mainnet.

Cardano Pie allocation:

- 20,000 ADA
- equal split among qualified participants

The Cardano Pie is sponsored by the Cardano Foundation.

Qualified Cardano Pie projects also get an opportunity to pitch to the Draper Dragon VC Fund.

## Requirements

To qualify for the Cardano Pie, a project must meet all of the following requirements.

### 1. Satisfy Every Builder Pie Requirement

The project must satisfy every requirement in the [Builder Pie Rules](builder-pie-rules.md).

This includes:

- building a user-facing software product
- including a clear payment gate
- registering every team member
- maintaining one official public repository
- starting with an empty repository
- posting weekly progress tweets
- making the product publicly accessible
- delivering a complete final presentation

### 2. Build on Cardano Mainnet

The project must deliver real functionality on Cardano mainnet.

The Cardano functionality must be part of the product participants present for verification.

### 3. Provide Cardano Mainnet Evidence

Participants must provide enough public evidence to verify that the product uses Cardano mainnet functionality.

Examples may include:

- mainnet transaction links
- deployed contract or policy IDs
- wallet interaction evidence
- public product flows that use Cardano mainnet
- repository code that supports the presented Cardano functionality

### 4. Complete Final Verification

The final presentation must include:

- a live demo of the product
- the official public repository link
- links to all required weekly tweets
- a deployed public product link
- evidence of Cardano mainnet functionality

## Payout Relationship to Builder Pie

The Cardano Pie replaces the Builder Pie for teams shipping qualifying Cardano projects.

A project that qualifies for the Cardano Pie receives a share of the Cardano Pie instead of the Builder Pie.

## Practical Standard

Cardano Pie qualification is based on visible, consistent, verifiable participation and real Cardano mainnet functionality.
