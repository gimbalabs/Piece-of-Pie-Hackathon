# Feedback Rules

## Feedback Pie Summary

The Feedback Pie rewards people who help builders improve through live, recorded interaction.

Feedback Pie allocation:

- 1,000 ADA
- distributed proportionally by valid feedback credits

## Who Can Participate

People may participate in the Feedback track even if they are not builders.

Builders are also encouraged to participate.

## Requirements

### 1. Feedback Must Happen Live

Feedback must be given through a live interaction, such as:

- Zoom
- Google Meet
- another comparable live meeting tool

### 2. Feedback Must Be Recorded

Each qualifying interaction must be recorded.

### 3. One Session Equals One Credit

Each qualifying feedback interaction counts as one credit.

### 4. Maximum Credits

Each participant may earn up to 15 credits.

### 5. Evidence Must Be Shareable

Feedback evidence must be available for verification.

Participants may use this public evidence template:

- https://docs.google.com/document/d/1FPellYxfgDSqw2Qlv6srPxh4DfKfjP20RHKns5OXvqg/edit?tab=t.0

## Payout Logic

The 1,000 ADA pool is divided proportionally across all valid credits earned by qualified participants.

## Practical Standard

The goal of this track is to reward helpful, visible, real interaction that improves builder projects.
