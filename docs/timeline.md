# Timeline

## 2026 Schedule

The dates below reflect the current 2026 schedule.

- Enrollment period: April 13, 2026 to April 26, 2026
- Build period: April 13, 2026 to July 5, 2026
- Final presentations: June 29, 2026 to July 5, 2026
- Payout window: July 5, 2026 to July 12, 2026

## Timeline by Phase

### Weeks 1-2

- Enrollment is open
- Each project registers once
- Every team member must be listed
- The official public repository must be ready

### Weeks 1-12

- Participants build in public
- At least one qualifying weekly progress tweet is required every week
- Builders should maintain visible commit history in the official public repository

### Final Presentation Window

During the final presentation window, qualifying builders must present:

- a live demo of the product
- the official public repository
- links to all required weekly tweets
- the deployed public product link

### Post-Hackathon

- verification is completed
- payout amounts are calculated by track
- qualified participants receive distributions according to the published rules

## Notes

- Enrollment closes after April 26, 2026.
- New entries are not accepted after the enrollment period closes.
